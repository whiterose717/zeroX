Cybersecurity Certifications

Advance your career with recognized cybersecurity credentials

Cybersecurity certifications validate your skills and knowledge in specific security domains. They can help you stand out in the job market, command higher salaries, and gain access to more advanced positions. Below are some of the most respected certifications in the industry.

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

EC-Council

Validates skills in identifying vulnerabilities and weaknesses in target systems using the same knowledge and tools as malicious hackers.

Intermediate Exam: 125 questions
Learn More

CompTIA Security+

CompTIA

Foundational cybersecurity certification covering network security, compliance, threats and vulnerabilities.

Beginner Exam: 90 questions
Learn More

CISSP

(ISC)²

Advanced certification demonstrating expertise in designing, implementing, and managing cybersecurity programs.

Advanced Exam: 100-150 questions
Learn More

OSCP

Offensive Security

Hands-on penetration testing certification requiring candidates to successfully attack and penetrate various live machines.

Advanced 24-hour practical exam
Learn More

CISM

ISACA

Focuses on information security governance, risk management, and program development and management.

Advanced Exam: 150 questions
Learn More

GSEC

GIAC

Validates a practitioner's knowledge of information security beyond simple terminology and concepts.

Intermediate Exam: 180 questions
Learn More
